The 2017 Collaboration Prize

The Collaboration Prize is a national competition that recognizes and celebrates nonprofit organizations that collaborate effectively to create greater impact.

Learn More

Celebrating nonprofits that come together for greater impact

The Collaboration Prize shines a spotlight on nonprofit organizations that use collaborative models as innovative and effective response to challenges or opportunities.

Apply Today

why the prize matters

The Prize celebrates nonprofit organizations that have chosen collaboration, rewards those that have achieved outstanding results, and offers models to inspire others. The Prize also raises awareness of collaboration as an effective strategy to improve outcomes, achieve scale, increase efficiency, and better serve the social goals of the organizations involved in the collaboration.

 
 

the lasting impact

In addition to identifying and showcasing exceptional nonprofit collaboration efforts, the Collaboration Prize collects and shares models and best practices through the Foundation Center’s Nonprofit Collaboration Database, a resource to inspire the sector to consider collaboration as a way to maximize impact.

The Reward

The Collaboration Prize will award a grand prize of $150,000. In addition, each of the eight finalists will receive $10,000.

Who is eligible to enter the Collaboration Prize?

• A permanent collaboration must have been formed by at least 2 nonprofit organizations

• A written formal agreement must exist that addresses the critical elements of the collaboration

• The collaboration must have been in operation for at least 18 months

• The collaboration must do most of its work in the United States and all parties to the collaboration must be located in the United States
